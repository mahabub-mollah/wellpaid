About Us

Our Mission

According to theÂ US Bureau of Labor Statistics, the mean hourly wage for maids and housekeeping cleaners in Washington , DC is a meager $13.32/hour.

Furthermore, many cleaning companies are misclassifying their employees as contractors, ensuring that workers are paid even less while lacking access to unemployment insurance, sick leave, vacation and health insurance. Companies then often require contract employees to use their own vehicles and supplirs, resulting in an even lower real hourly wage.

With nearly 15,000 people working as maids in our region, we believe we can help raise wages for everyone by demonstrating that respect for workers

Our Standards

At Well-Paid Maids, we pay all of our workers a living wage (per MIT's Living Wage Calculator) and offer a full benefits package. All employees start with the following wage and benefits:

  • $16/hour wage
  • CareFirst health and dental insurance
  • 5 paid sick days
  • 10 days of vacation
